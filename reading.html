Announcement

We invite all students and researchers in the area of computational linguistics to a new reading group about Dependency Grammar (DG). We will start with focusing on the relation between dependency syntax and semantics.

What is the aim of this reading group? At the moment, a number of people are about to write their dissertations (Stefan Thater and Ralph Debusmann) or diploma thesis (Christian Korthals) in the area of Dependency Grammar. With this reading group, we intend to get even more people interested in this area and share our ideas.

This reading group is connected with the CHORUS- and NEGRA-projects, and more specifically with Joachim Niehren, Denys Duchier, Geert-Jan Kruijff and Valia Kordoni.

We will meet every two weeks.

To join the reading group, please send an email to the organizer Ralph Debusmann rade@ps.uni-sb.de to register.
