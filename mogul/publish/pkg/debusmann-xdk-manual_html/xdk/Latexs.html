9.24 Latexs

This section explains the Latexs output functor.

  • identifier: output.latexs
  • open method: prints the dags corresponding to all dimensions using a graph principle (excluding e.g. the lex dimension). The individual dags are printed as by the Latex output functor (Latex).
  • close method: does nothing
