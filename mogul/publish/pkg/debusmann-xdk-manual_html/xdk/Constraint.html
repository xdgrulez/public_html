4.9.3 Constraint

Constraints are useful for generic principles such as principle.nodeconstraints and principle.edgeconstraints.

Currently, we support six constraints which are all binary, and are written as follows:

  • equality: equal(<term_1> <term_2>)
  • distinctness: notequal(<term_1> <term_2>)
  • inclusion: include(<term_1> <term_2>)
  • exclusion: exclude(<term_1> <term_2>)
  • subset: subseteq(<term_1> <term_2>)
  • disjointness: disjoint(<term_1> <term_2>)
  • non-disjointness: notdisjoint(<term_1> <term_2>)
