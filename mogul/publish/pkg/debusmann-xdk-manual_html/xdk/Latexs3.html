9.27 Latexs3

This section explains the Latexs3 output functor.

  • identifier: output.latexs3
  • open method: prints the dags corresponding to all dimensions using a graph principle (excluding e.g. the lex dimension). The individual dags are printed as by the Latex3 output functor (Latex3).
  • close method: does nothing
