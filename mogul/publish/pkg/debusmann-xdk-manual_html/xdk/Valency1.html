7.2.69 Valency principle

  • identifier: principle.valency
  • dimension variables: D
  • argument variables:
    In: valency(label(D))
    Out: valency(label(D))
  • default values:
    In: _.D.entry.in
    Out: _.D.entry.out
  • model record: empty
  • constraints: In1 (priority 130), Out (priority 130)

This principle combines the In1 principle (In1) and the Out principle (Out) in one, and is thus easier to use.
