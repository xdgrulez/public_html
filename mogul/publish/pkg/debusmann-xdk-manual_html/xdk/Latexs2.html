9.26 Latexs2

This section explains the Latexs2 output functor.

  • identifier: output.latexs2
  • open method: prints the dags corresponding to all dimensions using a graph principle (excluding e.g. the lex dimension). The individual dags are printed as by the Latex2 output functor (Latex2).
  • close method: does nothing
