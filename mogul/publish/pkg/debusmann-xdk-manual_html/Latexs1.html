9.25 Latexs1

This section explains the Latexs1 output functor.

  • identifier: output.latexs1
  • open method: prints the dags corresponding to all dimensions using a graph principle (excluding e.g. the lex dimension). The individual dags are printed as by the Latex1 output functor (Latex1).
  • close method: does nothing
